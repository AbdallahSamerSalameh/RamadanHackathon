Event Details

Our annual Community Iftar is a special gathering that brings together people from all walks of life to share in the blessings of Ramadan. This is a wonderful opportunity to strengthen community bonds, meet new friends, and enjoy a delicious meal together as we break our fast.

The event will feature a welcome address from our charity director, a short talk about the significance of Ramadan, and of course, a communal iftar meal with a variety of traditional dishes. We will also have a short program after the meal including nasheeds (Islamic songs) performed by local children.

All are welcome to attend, regardless of faith or background. This is a family-friendly event, and children's activities will be available.
